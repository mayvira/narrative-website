Day one

On the first day of my arrival, after arriving at Bristol Temple Meads train station, I visited the Bristol Cathedral, College Green, then walked around the central harbourside, Bristol bridge and ended the day with watching the sunset at the city docks.

Arrival - Bristol Temple Meads

I travelled to Bristol by train, arriving at Bristol Temple Meads station, which is relatively close to the city centre.

College Green

College Green is a public park between the Bristol City Council and Bristol Cathedral. Its location in the city centre makes it a popular space for people to relax, picnic, read or peoplewatch.

City Walk

After that, I took a stroll around the city centre, familiarising myself with the streets and different areas.

Nice Spice Chinese Restaurant

After a lot of walking, I stopped for dinner at Nice Spice, a Chinese Restaurant in a little alley near the central harbour. They have amazing Char Siu - Chinese Style Roasted Pork on rice.

Bristol City Docks

To end the day, I went to see the sunset at the city docks, which was close to the hotel I was staying in. There were several restaurants and shops overlooking the dock, and seating areas where you can take in the beautiful sunset view, great way to end the first day in this city.
