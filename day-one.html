let's get started
Day one

On the first day of my arrival, after arriving at Bristol Temple Meads train station, I visited the Bristol Cathedral, College Green, then walked around the central harbourside, Bristol bridge and ended the day with watching the sunset at the city docks.

I travelled to Bristol by train, arriving at Bristol Temple Meads station, which is relatively close to the city centre.

Bristol Cathedral

Bristol Cathedral is a historical hall church in the city centre, "one of England's great medieval churches" since the twelveth century. It has gorgeous architecture, both interior and exterior, with an award-winning outdoor garden inside. (Bristol Cathedral, n.d.)

College Green

After admiring the cathedral, I came out to college green, it is a public park between the Bristol City Council and Bristol Cathedral. Its location in the city centre makes it a popular space for students and nearby residents to relax, picnic, read and peoplewatch.

City Walk

After that, I took a stroll around the city centre, familiarising myself with the streets and different areas. The photo to the left is me walking on Bristol Bridge.

Nice Spice Chinese Restaurant

I stopped for dinner at Nice Spice, a Chinese Restaurant in a little alley near the central harbour. They have amazing Char Siu - Chinese Style Roasted Pork on rice.

Bristol City Docks

To end the day, I went to see the sunset at the city docks, which was close to the hotel I was staying in. The gorgeous sunset view was a great way to end my first day.
