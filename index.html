May's Bristol Diary

What's this website about?

On this website, I will be bringing you through my journey of Bristol over 5 days, spanning over art galleries, museum, exhibitions, parks, markets and more. I hope this website can be a good guide for someone who wants a casual guide to the Bristol city centre area, whilst also a travel journal that I can look back to.

Getting there How do I travel there?

A train to Bristol Temple Meads Station will get you close to the city centre.

Transport How do I get around the city?

For me, Bristol is a very walkable city, although relatively hilly, it was fun to explore the city on foot. The most popular method of transport for both locals and visitors is Voi Electronic Scooters, they are scattered around the city and are easy to use and pay for. Bicycles are the second popular.
